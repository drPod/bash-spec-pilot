The implementation uses only libc for Unix ownership calls and filetime for portable setting of atime/mtime; argument parsing and traversal are implemented directly to keep the crate single-file and predictable. It covers common GNU cp behavior including recursive copies, symlink modes, overwrite/update/backup prompts, hard/symbolic links, parents, verbose output, and basic preservation, while intentionally skipping SELinux, ACL/xattr, sparse/reflink internals, and special-device recreation.
